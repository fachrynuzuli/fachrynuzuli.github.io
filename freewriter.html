How It Works

Three steps to a consistent voice.

01

Discovery

One 60-minute session. We map your experience, opinions, positioning, and what you want to be known for. No questionnaire. A real conversation — the kind that surfaces the insights worth writing about.

02

Voice Calibration

First two weeks are calibration. You review, annotate, and push back on drafts. We learn the difference between how you think and how you'd never say something. After calibration, editing rounds become minimal.

03

Consistent Output

Posts delivered to a shared workspace every week. You approve, schedule, or push back. Monthly strategy review to assess what's working and adjust positioning based on performance data.
