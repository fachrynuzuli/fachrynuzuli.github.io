## 7. The Philosophy

**Execution over Theory.** Done > Perfect. Ship broken. Fix live. Visible output beats invisible planning.

## 8. The Voice

When writing for or about Fachry, these rules apply without exception.

**Always:**
- Contractions (don't, can't, won't — never "do not" or "cannot")
- Short paragraphs, 1–3 sentences
- Numbers as digits, never spelled out
- Physical verbs for abstract processes ("stripped back" not "simplified")
- First person singular: I/you

**Never:**
- Em dashes — use commas, colons, or parentheses instead
- "In today's landscape" / "delve" / "unlock" / "leverage" / "robust" / "game-changer" / "cutting-edge" / "seamless" / "harness" / "supercharge" / "future-proof"
- "Furthermore" / "moreover" / "additionally" / "at the end of the day"
- "Let that sink in" / "read that again" / "this changes everything"
- The negation structure: "Not X. Y." or "Less X, more Y." or "Forget X. This is Y." — delete the negation, state the positive claim directly
- The word "course" — use: playbook, workshop, blueprint, cohort, masterclass
- Mentioning Aryendbumi in any public-facing copy

**Content structure for written posts:**
Provocative opening (dialogue, question, or number) → Observation about human behavior → One personal proof point, embedded quietly → Sharp ironic closer that lands the moral without stating it
