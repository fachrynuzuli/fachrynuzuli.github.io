How the Algorithm Actually Works (What You Need to Know)

### The Credit Score System
Every tweet either builds or damages your reputation. No neutral.
- Good engagement = algorithm trusts you = more reach
- Zero engagement = low quality signal = death spiral

### Your First 30 Minutes Are EVERYTHING
Engagement velocity in first 30 min determines if tweet lives or dies.
- Strong early engagement → algorithm pushes harder
- Flat start → tweet dies

Your solution: Need core engagement group who replies/RTs immediately (more on this below)

### What the Algorithm Values (in order)
1. Retweets/Quote Tweets - Exposes to new audiences (best)
2. Replies - Signals conversation (makes posts pop off)
3. Profile clicks - MASSIVE boost (algorithm says "people want to follow this person")
4. Dwell time - How long people read (stop-scroll = positive signal)
5. Likes/Bookmarks - Good but weakest signal

### The Relationship Graph
Algorithm maps how connected users are based on interactions.
- Tweet hits immediate graph first
- Strong early engagement → expands to similar audiences
- This is how tweets go 100 views → 100k views

Critical insight: When you have 0 followers, algorithm doesn't know WHO to show your content to. This is why you need outside push.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

### 1. Posting Without Outside Push
"Just posting great content" doesn't work at 800 followers. Algorithm doesn't know who to show it to. You'll stay stuck.

Solution: Get affiliate or paid engagement locked in FIRST, then post.

### 2. Over-Posting Low-Quality Content
7 mediocre posts < 3 excellent posts. Quality wins when you're small.

Solution: 5 posts/week, every one must be valuable.

### 3. Not Being Online First 30 Minutes
If you post then disappear, early engagement dies, tweet dies.

Solution: Post when you can be online for 30 min after.

### 4. Ignoring Your Engagement Pod
If your 30-Day Challenge group isn't engaging, your tweets won't get initial velocity.

Solution: Make engagement pod participation mandatory for challenge.

### 5. Philosophy Without Proof
X rewards concrete proof over abstract wisdom.

Solution: Every philosophy post needs attached build/proof.

### 6. Separating Yourself from Audience
Article warns: Don't make Naval's mistake (talking AT audience from mountain).

Solution: "I was stuck at 800 followers last month. Here's what I learned..." (still in the arena with them)

### 7. Posting Same Content to All Platforms
X rewards native text threads. Instagram rewards carousels. Don't cross-post.

Solution: Adapt format per platform. Focus X first.
